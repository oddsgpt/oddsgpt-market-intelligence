# What Is Betting Market Intelligence?

Betting Market Intelligence is the study of how information becomes probability within betting markets.

Rather than focusing solely on match predictions, market intelligence examines:

* Closing Odds
* Market Movements
* Sharp Money Activity
* Closing Line Value (CLV)
* Market Efficiency
* Probability Modeling
* AI Betting Systems

The objective is to understand how football markets process information and how market prices evolve over time.

# AI Championship

The OddsGPT AI Championship is an ongoing public research project where multiple AI betting systems compete using real football betting markets.

Unlike traditional tipster competitions, all systems operate under identical market conditions and are evaluated using objective performance metrics.

Tracked metrics include:

* ROI
* Win Rate
* Closing Line Value (CLV)
* League-Level Performance
* Market Movement
* Long-Term Profitability

The purpose is not simply to identify winning selections.

The objective is to study which forecasting philosophies consistently create value relative to football betting markets.

Learn more:

https://www.oddsgpt.com/ai-championship/en
